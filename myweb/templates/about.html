Meet Pelumi

I am Oguntola Pelumi Joshua, a certified Data Scientist passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive better decisions. I graduated from the Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Information and Informatics at Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, Ogbomoso.

With a strong passion for data and analytical thinking, I enrolled in the Data Science professional program at SQI College of ICT in April 2024, where I developed solid skills as a Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and Full-Stack Developer. I successfully completed the program with an Excellent grade and received my professional certification in Dec 2025.

Alongside my analytical expertise, I build modern and responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and develop powerful, scalable backend systems with the Python Django Framework. I also specialize in data mining, extracting data from multiple sources and transforming them into structured datasets for meaningful insights and informed decision-making.
